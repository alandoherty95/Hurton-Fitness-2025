About Iain Hurton

A supportive, experienced coach helping people of all ages build confidence through fitness.

I love all kinds of exercise including swimming, lifting weights, running and hiking. Fitness has been my passion since I was 16 years old.

Through personal training, group classes, and bootcamps, my goal is simple: help people feel stronger, healthier, and more confident — without intimidation.

Qualifications & Certifications

  • Advanced Sports Recreation and Exercise (LCFE)
  • Sport, Recreation and Exercise (CCL)
  • Business Management (QQI Level 5)
  • PFS Kettlebells
  • PFS Aqua Fitness Instructor
  • PFS Indoor Cycling Instructor
